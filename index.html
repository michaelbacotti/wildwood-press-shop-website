Wildwood Press Shop

A print shop in the wildwoods.

Modern graphic design studio creating print-on-demand art, apparel, and decor for curious, creative people.

I'm Mike, the designer behind Wildwood Press Shop — a small graphic design studio experimenting with bold, modern artwork across many styles. From clean minimalism and geometric abstractions to retro vibes and experimental AI-assisted pieces, I love exploring new looks so your space (or wardrobe) never feels generic.

Every design starts in my New Hampshire studio, where I sketch, refine, and prepare artwork specifically for high-quality print-on-demand production. I work with Printful to produce and ship each piece on demand, which means less waste, consistent quality, and a wide range of options — from wall art and posters to apparel and accessories.

My goal is to create art that feels personal, thoughtful, and a little bit unexpected — whether you're decorating your home office, gifting a friend, or refreshing your everyday style. When you order from this shop, you're supporting an independent creator and getting a design made with intention, not a mass-market template.

If you're looking for a specific color palette, style, or niche theme, feel free to send me a message. I'm always open to custom work and new ideas.

— Mike, Wildwood Press Shop
